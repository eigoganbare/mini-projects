How to Use the Library

View the library and send the Librarian an email via our online contact form. In your email, please include:

  • Author
  • Title
  • Mailing address

There will most likely be a delay of one or two weeks before the books are shipped.


How to Use the Library at a Retreat

Find some books that you like, and write your information on the check-out list. Done! Enjoy your books!

How to Return Books

When you are finished with your materials, please:

  • Bring it to the next retreat
  • Mail it back to the Librarian

The return address will be provided to you when you request materials from the library.

Due Dates

Technically, there are no due dates. However, we do request that you return books after you finish reading them or after a year or two if it is just collecting dust on your shelf.

DO NOT LEAVE JAPAN UNTIL YOU RETURN YOUR LIBRARY BOOKS!!!
